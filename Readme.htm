Mars Time-Explore


　　This simulation game gives you a chance to drive rover to explore Mars.You could search for some valuable thread objects,such as sphere-like grains or "blueberries", regarded as the proof of water on Mars.You could get the news thread only if the rover contacts the objects.Then you could send these news to the earth,NASA will pay you back as a reward.
　　There are some supply station on Mars.You could get some supply there,such as purchasing energy block and upgrading the part of rover.

　　The objective is just to find the most valuable thread object,which is normally mentioned at the beginning of the mission.

Features

　　1.This simulation game give the player a chance to explore Mars.The Scene is filled with rich terrains and threads.The greatest joy of the game is exploration.

　　2.The supply station and thread objects could appear at different places,which randomize the game process and avoid the repetitious game play.

　　3.The supply station could provide some information about the environment.Player could determine the exploration direction according these reference.

　　4.The part of rover could be upgraded at supply station.For example, player could upgrade solar battery to improve the electricity transfer efficiency.

　　5.The rover just survive on Mars,because the energy and health is very important to the rover.You must assure the sufficient energy and health.
